# ArdiuNooo
- A collection of arduino sketches made by someone who had a breadboard, a pile of sensors, and too much free time.
- think of it like a group of friends handed a bunch of instruments 🎷🥁— whether or not it makes music is beside the point, we're just seeing what happens.
- here for the weird ideas, the senseless combinations, and giving stories to things that probably shouldn't have stories. 🌀
-  went from fumbling through my first class absolutely terrified 😭 to creating my own disco lights on weekends — with a smoke sensor on standby. not a bad idea you see 🪩🚨
